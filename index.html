Our Story

The spirit of the kopitiam, reimagined

Hang Tuah Kopi & Toastery was born in 2016 with a simple idea: bring the timeless warmth of the Singaporean kopitiam into modern Jakarta life. We named ourselves after the legendary Malay warrior — a symbol of loyalty, resilience, and belonging.

Our Dewi Sartika branch at Cawang carries that same spirit. Settle in for a kaya butter toast with a perfectly pulled kopi tarik, or come for a full meal of Hainanese chicken rice or laksa mee. The doors are open, every day.

The Kopitiam Concept

More than a café — a way of life

The kopitiam — or "coffee shop" in Hokkien-Malay — has been the social heartbeat of Singapore and the Malay Peninsula for over a century. A place where dawn workers share a table with office-goers, where conversations linger as long as the kopi is hot.

Hang Tuah brings that culture to Jakarta: accessible, welcoming, unhurried. Our Modern Living Kopitiam concept honours tradition while fitting into your everyday urban rhythm — whether you're grabbing breakfast before the commute or sitting in for a long, slow lunch.

🏡
Indoor & Outdoor Seating
AC indoor zone for focused work; outdoor area for the casual smoker or fresh-air lover.
🌿
Relaxed Atmosphere
Never rushed. Come for 30 minutes or three hours — you'll always feel at home.
🤝
Community First — No Pork, No Lard, Always
Since day one, Hang Tuah has been committed to a fully Muslim-friendly experience. Every dish and every drink on our menu is prepared without pork or lard, so everyone is welcome at the table.
